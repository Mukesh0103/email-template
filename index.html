When physical distance between your teams gets to you, AnywhereWorks lets your team members catch up with each other via video calls. Host group meetings with up to 20 participants online, without leaving the app.
1. Navigate to video icon in the sidebar of your AnywhereWorks app.
2. Click the “Start” button to start a video chat room.
3. Next, click on the “Invite members” button in the chat room.
4. Enter the name of your peers to invite them to the video chat or get a shareable link and pass it on to your team members.
To learn more, email us at dev.aw@anywhereworks.com or start an instant chat via the chat widget on our homepage.
